👋 歡迎來到我的個人網頁

我擁有二十多年業務及管理經驗，熟悉經銷商、量販、超市、便利商店等各大通路操作。曾領導多個銷售團隊達成業績目標，具備策略規劃與現場執行力。面對市場快速變化與消費者行為轉型，我積極投入數位行銷與跨領域整合，提升團隊靈活應變與創新競爭力。未來將持續精進，掌握市場脈動，拓展更多發展機會。

除了業務與管理專業，我對威士忌與葡萄酒的品飲有長年且系統性的涉獵。深入理解單一麥芽威士忌的蒸餾工藝、橡木桶熟成變化，以及葡萄酒的品種、產區與年份特性，是我長時間品味與學習的積累。我特別擅長將酒款的風味曲線與料理的香氣層次精準對應，打造出和諧且令人驚豔的餐酒搭配體驗。這不僅是一種生活美學，更是一種溝通力 —— 在品牌體驗、顧客互動與高端接待中，我常以這份感知力與文化深度，營造出更有溫度與深度的交流場域。

I have over 20 years of experience in sales and management, with deep expertise in operating across major channels such as wholesaler, hypermarkets, supermarkets, and convenience stores. I have led multiple sales teams to achieve performance goals, excelling in strategic planning and frontline execution. In response to rapidly changing market dynamics and evolving consumer behaviors, I actively pursue digital marketing and cross-domain integration to enhance team adaptability and innovation. Moving forward, I aim to continuously sharpen my skills, stay aligned with market trends, and seize new growth opportunities.

Beyond business and management, I have cultivated a deep and structured knowledge of whisky and wine tasting over the years. I possess a thorough understanding of single malt whisky distillation techniques, barrel aging transformations, and the varietal and regional nuances of wine across vintages. My strength lies in precisely pairing the sensory profiles of spirits and wines with culinary elements to create harmonious and memorable dining experiences. This is not only a lifestyle aesthetic but also a refined communication skill—enhancing brand experiences, customer interactions, and premium hospitality with a touch of cultural depth and emotional resonance.
